## Analyses performed

### Model with the highest revenue
#### Insight and observations
- Iphone14 pro generated the highest revenue. This may be because it has the quality the clients want and it is affordable

### Country with highest revenue
#### Insight and observation
- USA generated the highest revenue. This shows that the country loves iPhone products

### Country that bought the highest quantity
#### Insight and observation
- UK bought the highest quantity. It was also observed that they generated the highest revenue after USA. This shows they also shows interest in iPhone products
  
### Most sold colour
#### Insight
- Blue is the most sold colour
  
### Most used payment method
#### Insight
- Debit card is the most used payment method
  
### Top performing month
- January is the top performing month

## Business performance
-	The monthly trend shows that the quantity of the product sold and the revenue generated keeps decreasing over the year. This may be due wrong target audience, location.
